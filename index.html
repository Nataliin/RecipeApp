Ingredients:

  • - 350-400 g of pork (shoulder or back part)
  • - 1 carrot
  • - 3 bulbs
  • - 2 peas of black pepper
  • - 2 sweet peas
  • - 400 g of white cabbage
  • - 4-5 potatoes
  • - 2-3 st. l. sunflower oil
  • - 1 package of beet-tomato borscht dressing from Torchyn
  • - 2.5-3 liters of water
  • - 200 g of cream cheese or sour cream (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Step 1. Remove the films from 350-400 g of pork. Cut into large portions.
  2. Step 2. Heat the pan with 2-3 tbsp. l. of sunflower oil, transfer the meat and fry it well on all sides until golden brown. We spent 10 minutes on it.
  3. Step 3. Transfer the fried pieces to a saucepan, add 3 onions, cut crosswise, one peeled carrot cut in half, and 2 peas each of fragrant and black pepper. Pour 2.5-3 liters of water and cook for 30 minutes, periodically removing the foam with a spoon.
  4. Step 4. Peel 4-5 potatoes and cut them into equal medium pieces, and chop 400 g of white cabbage into small strips. Remove the onion and carrot from the broth and transfer the chopped vegetables. Add half of the beetroot-tomato dressing from Torchyn. Cook until the potatoes are ready. We spent 20 minutes on it.
  5. Step 5. Add the remaining dressing, salt to taste and cook for another 5 minutes. Remove the borscht from the heat, let it infuse for 10 minutes.
  6. Step 6. Serve with sour cream or cream cheese. And sprinkle freshly chopped greens on top.
